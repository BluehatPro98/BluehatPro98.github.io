Current day child labor stats

Work and Death

Acording to the GFA, about 2.78 million children have died in work-related incidents and about 374 million have been injuried. Along with that, Bangladesh is the worst country when it comes to child labor. Using children for garment factories, farming, and manufacturing. The Bureau of International Labor Affairs provides this graph showing what children in Bangladesh are doing, how old they are, and the percentage of them doing it:

Not all bad...

The Bureau of International Labor Affairs states that,“Since 2017, government programs have removed 90,000 children from hazardous labor conditions, and over 35,000 children from exploitative work. Within 15 ministries responsible for children in some way, the government of Bangladesh increased its spending on children by 17 percent between the 2017-2018 and 2018-2019 fiscal years.”
